About Me

My name is Loren Kolstad. I love listening to music, going on hikes, spending time with friends and family, and staying busy.

I am allergic to cats, yet I have two. I live downtown, visit coffee shops way too frequently, and love going to live shows. I listen to all sorts of music, so if you have suggestions, please let me know (unless it’s Country.. no offense). I really enjoy photography, so if any of you either like to be in front of the camera, or also like to take photos, scroll down to the bottom of the page and “Say Hello!”. Additionally, I spend a lot of my free time outdoors and traveling. Some of my favorite places are South Yuba, Carmel, Stinson, Belize, and the Virgin Islands (so far). I am ecstatic to be learning coding, as I hope it brings me the freedom to work from my laptop doing something that I won’t get bored with after a few years.
